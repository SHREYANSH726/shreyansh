WELCOME TO PATANG
RE-EVOLVE!

Inspired by the 'Chabutaro, a traditional tower-like structure where birds are
fed,' NeelAsmita Re-Evolving's unique design draws a profound connection
to the rich heritage of Ahmedabad since the past four decades. It gracefully
rotates 221 feet above the ground, on the banks of the Sabarmati River,
while offering a panoramic view of both old and new Ahmedabad.


As an emblem of Ahmedabad's skyline, this revolving icon has been reborn
with a refined ambiance and culinary renaissance, while emerging as a
symbol of timeless charm. We cordially invite you to be part of this new
chapter, where excellence is our tradition and hospitality is our legacy.
